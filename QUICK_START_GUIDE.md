### Step 1: Sign Up & Choose Your Role

1. Visit Renowise homepage
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Select your role:
   - **Homeowner**: Post materials, request services, manage projects
   - **Company**: Offer services, buy materials, manage orders
   - **City**: Manage municipal resources and spaces

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

- Add your name and contact information
- For Companies: Add company name, description, logo
- For Cities: Add city name and municipal details
- Verify your email (if required)

### Step 3: Explore the Map

- The map is your central interface
- Browse available posts in your area
- Click pins to see post details
- Use search to find specific items

### Step 4: Create Your First Post

**For Homeowners:**
1. Click **"Create Post"** in left menu
2. Choose: Material / Service / Space
3. Fill in details, upload images (2-6 required)
4. Set your location (required!)
5. Submit

**For Companies:**
1. Same process as homeowners
2. Focus on services you offer
3. Set competitive rates

**For Cities:**
1. Post municipal materials or spaces
2. Set availability dates
3. Specify permit requirements

### Step 5: Interact with Posts

**Making Offers (Companies):**
1. Browse map or "Available Posts"
2. Click a post you're interested in
3. Click **"Make an Offer"**
4. Enter amount and message
5. Submit

**Managing Offers (Post Owners):**
1. Go to "Projects & Offers" â†’ "Offers Received"
2. Review offers
3. Accept or reject

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I make an offer on my own post?**  
A: No, the system prevents self-offers.

**Q: How do I change my offer?**  
A: Go to "Offers Made", click on pending offer, modify amount/message.

**Q: What happens when I accept an offer?**  
A: It becomes an order, post status changes to RESERVED.

**Q: Can I access other role dashboards?**  
A: No, each role can only access their own dashboard.

**Q: How do I center the map on a post?**  
A: Click the post in any list, map automatically centers.
